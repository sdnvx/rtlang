= Operators

== Arithmetic operators

[cols="3,1"]
|===
| Operation | Syntax

| Addition          | `a + b`
| Subtraction       | `a - b`
| Unary plus        | `+a`
| Unary minus       | `-a`
| Multiplication    | `a * b`
| Division          | `a * b`
| Modulo            | `a % b`
| Power             | `a ** b`
| Prefix increment  | `++a`
| Postfix increment | `a++`
| Prefix decrement  | `--a`
| Postfix decrement | `a--`
|===

=== Relational operators

[cols="3,1"]
|===
| Operation | Syntax

| Equal to                 | `a == b`
| Not equal to             | `a != b`
| Less than                | `a < b`
| Less than or equal to    | `a <= b`
| Greater than             | `a > b`
| Greater than or equal to | `a >= b`
|===

=== Logical operators

[cols="3,1"]
|===
| Operation | Syntax

| Logical negation              | `not a`
| Logical conjunction           | `a and b`
| Logical disjunction           | `a or b`
| Logical exclusive disjunction | `a xor b`
|===

== Bitwise operators

[cols="3,1"]
|===
| Operation | Syntax

| Bitwise negation              | `~a`
| Bitwise conjunction           | `a & b`
| Bitwise disjunction           | `a \| b`
| Bitwise exclusive disjunction | `a ^ b`
| Shift left                    | `a << b`
| Circular shift left           | `a <<< b`
| Shift right                   | `a >> b`
| Circular shift right          | `a >>> b`
|===

== Assignment operators

[cols="3,1"]
|===
| Operation | Syntax

| Assignment                                | `a := b`
| Addition combination                      | `a += b`
| Subtraction combination                   | `a -= b`
| Multiplication combination                | `a *= b`
| Division combination                      | `a /= b`
| Modulo combination                        | `a %= b`
| Power combination                         | `a **= b`
| Bitwise negation combination              | `a ~= b`
| Bitwise conjunction combination           | `a &= b`
| Bitwise disjunction combination           | `a \|= b`
| Butwise exclusive disjunction combination | `a ^= b`
| Bitwise left shift combination            | `a <\<= b`
| Bitwise circrular left shift combination  | `a <<\<= b`
| Bitwise right shift combination           | `a >>= b`
| Bitwise circular right shift combination  | `a >>>= b`
|===

== Member and pointer operators

[cols="3,1"]
|===
| Operation | Syntax

| Subscript           | `a[b]`
| Indirection         | `*a`
| Structure reference | `a.b`
| Address of          | `@a`
|===

== Other operators

[cols="3,1"]
|===
| Operation | Syntax

| Function call       | `a(b)`
| Comma               | `a, b`
| Scope resolution    | `a::b`
| Conversion          | `R(a)`
|===

== Operators predecence

[cols="1,1,3,2"]
|===
| Order | Operator | Description | Associativity

|  1
| `::`
| Scope resolution
| None

| 2
| `++`
| Postfix increment
| Left-to-right

| 2
| `--`
| Postfix decrement
| Left-to-right

| 2
| `()`
| Function call
| Left-to-right

| 2
| `[]`
| Array subscripting
| Left-to-right

| 2
| `.`
| Element selection
| Left-to-right
|===
